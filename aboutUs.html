About Swiftie

Swiftie is an all-in-one marketplace where you can request the services of handymen and artisans close to your location from anywhere in the world in a few clicks.

Who we serve and what we offer

Swiftie serves as a mediator between customers looking for artisans and artisans looking for customers. As a result, swiftie serves everyone looking for a service provider or an artisan to offer a particular service and also serves artisans looking to make extra and to put their srrvices out to the public.

Swiftie offers artisans an online platform to advertise their craft and also earn. With nothing less than 20,000 customers, artisans are sure of getting their craft seen.

We also offer the best artisans to customers who are looking to get a task done or a service provided

Swiftie's principle

  • At Swiftie, honesty, openness, integrity, and superior craftsmanship are our guiding principles.
  • Honesty is the cornerstone of our business practices, not merely because it is the best policy. We don't defraud our customers or artisans.
  • Inclusion and Safety - Anyone who wants to use our platform to connect with artisans that specialize in a variety of crafts is welcome to do so. We guarantee our users' security.
  • Fairness - From clients to artisans, everyone at Swiftie is treated fairly and given their due.
  • Feedback - We value direct and honest communication and welcome frank and helpful criticism.
